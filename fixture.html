Fixture Design

Modern Indian Accessories Brand · Los Angeles, CA · Confidential

A merchandising fixture system combining metal and wood with flexible back rails — balancing the industrial character of steel with the warmth of wood, drawn from materials naturally rooted in the Indian context.

Designed and developed merchandising display fixtures for a modern Indian accessories brand. The system uses metal and wood with flexible back rails to support dynamic in-store arrangements — allowing the brand to reconfigure displays as product lines evolve.

The material pairing was intentional: the industrial character of steel grounds the fixtures structurally and visually, while wood introduces warmth and softness that references materials naturally rooted in the Indian craft tradition the brand draws from.
