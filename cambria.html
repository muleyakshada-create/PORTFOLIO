Cambria Hotel Lobby

Los Angeles — Spring Street · Adaptive Reuse · Hospitality

An adaptive reuse of a Downtown LA parking garage into a hotel in Gallery Row — where a locally inspired design narrative translates into the guest experience.

Contributed to the lobby design of the Cambria Hotel Los Angeles – Spring Street, an adaptive reuse project converting a parking garage into a hotel in Downtown LA's Gallery Row.

Developed renderings to visualize the spatial concept, materiality, and guest experience — supporting the translation of a locally inspired design narrative into the final built environment.
